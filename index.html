Introducing Rachel

I graduated with a Master of Arts in Mass Communication with a specialization in public relations from the University of Florida on May 2, 2014. My undergraduate experience was spent at The Ohio State University where I received a degree in strategic communications. The experience I’ve had at these universities academically and socially have helped shape me into the person I am today.

I had no idea I wanted to study public relations when I started college. My undergraduate major changed from pre-design to marketing, until I finally settled on strategic communications. The few classes I took focusing solely on public relations had me interested but it took me a while to realize that is what I needed to be doing. After I realized public relations was what I wanted to be doing, I decided to apply to the University of Florida’s public relations graduate program and go back to school.

Which brings me to this website. During my last semester at UF I took a semester long course where I was taught HTML and CSS. By the end of the semester I was required to have this website up and running using everything I learned in class. Not only is this site my portfolio it also shows a new skill I learned in those three months. This was my first attempt at a website but I’ve had lots of experience writing articles, press releases, creating communication plans, event planning, conducting research, developing creative material and managing social media accounts. Take a look around and let me know what you think.
