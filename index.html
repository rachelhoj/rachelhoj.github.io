Introducing Rachel

I graduated with a Master of Arts in Mass Communication with a specialization in public relations from the University of Florida on May 2, 2014. My undergraduate experience was spent at The Ohio State University where I received a degree in strategic communications. The experience that I have had at these universities academically and socially have helped shape me into the person I am today.

I did not start my educational journey knowing that I wanted to practice public relations in my adult life. My undergraduate major changed from pre-design to marketing, until I finally settled on strategic communications. The few classes I took focusing solely on public relations had me interested in pursuing it as a profession. I had only a small working knowledge of what public relations was so I decided to apply to the University of Floridaâ€™s public relations graduate program.

This website is my first attempt at HTML and CSS. The final product that you see here was conceptualized and completed during a three-month period. During this time, I learned what HTML and CSS are and coded for the first time in my life. Although this website is utilized as an online portfolio of sorts, it is also a product and showcases a new skill I learned during my last semester at the University of Florida.

My professional and educational experience includes writing press releases and articles, creating strategic public relations plans, event planning, conducting research studies, developing creative material and managing social media accounts.
